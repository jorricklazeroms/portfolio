# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free collaboration space for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Use respectful, professional language.
- Give actionable, technical feedback.
- Assume good intent and resolve conflicts constructively.
- Focus on project goals and shared accountability.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code. Repeated violations can lead to temporary or permanent bans from collaboration spaces.

## Reporting
Report incidents to project maintainers through private channels in your organization.
